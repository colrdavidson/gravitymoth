Spall Intro in 30 Seconds

  • Spall is a profiler for C, C++, and Odin
  • Visualiser scales to huge traces (100 GB+) at 60 fps
  • There's a free web version that is 10x faster than competitors like chrome://tracing
  • There's a paid native version with support for automatic and manual tracing

You Might Like Spall If

  • You're new to tracing and want an easy setup process
  • You spend a lot of time waiting for your current profiler to load your traces
  • You're tired of fishing through 15 nested popup boxes in your current profiler to find useful stats
